Frequently Asked Questions

We are open Mondays to Sunday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We are closed on Hoildays.

While we do accept walk-ins if there is availability, we highly recommend booking an appointment in advance to secure your preferred time and stylist, especially for longer services like coloring or treatments.

We kindly request at least 24 hours' notice for any cancellations or rescheduling. This allows us to offer the appointment slot to another client. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice may be subject to a fee.

We use a curated selection of high-quality, professional hair care products in our salon. You can browse and purchase our featured products directly from the "Shop" section on our website.
